Visualization Scripts
Guided visualizations are a powerful way to manifest positive
outcomes. They can also be used to alleviate anxiety or stress by generating transformative mental images, pulling in sights, sounds, scents, tastes, and feelings.
There are different degrees to visualization scripts:
1.Generate images
2.Create relevant context around the imagery
3.Call attention to details and vivid sensory descriptions
of object(s) in the image.
4.Where applicable, transform imagery from negative and painful images to positive and healthy images
