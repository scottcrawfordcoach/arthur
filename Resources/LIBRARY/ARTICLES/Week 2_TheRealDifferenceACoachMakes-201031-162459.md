Self-Management Is The Key
Great coaches practice humility. It's the magic ingredient. There is no room for ego in Coaching, and when the ego makes an appearance, self-management is the key. To go from good to great as

Being a professional coach means agreeing to hold space for another person. To listen without an agenda. To approach with curiosity and interest in the person's mindset- not the problem.
