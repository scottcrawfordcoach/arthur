What is Clean Language?
ICF Life & Wellness Coach

Psychotherapist David Grove developed the Clean
Language Technique in the 1980s, when he realized that
many therapists subtly influence what a client says, or
deems important during sessions.

The objective is for the coach to keep their own thoughts,
assumptions and metaphors out of the conversation by
removing suggestions and interpretations.
